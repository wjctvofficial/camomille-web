# Camomille — Brand Reference

Extracted from camomille.dev (Home / Services / Pricing screenshots) and `logo-gold.svg`.
Personality: calm, warm, trustworthy. "Chamomile" = peace of mind. The product is no-risk
custom web development paid in leasing ("Relax, we take the risk").

## Typography

Rounded, friendly geometric sans throughout.
- Headings: bold and rounded (Baloo 2 / Fredoka style).
- Body: humanist rounded sans (Nunito / Quicksand style).
- Colour hierarchy: gold for hero H1s, sage green for section headings, ink for body.

(Exact font families to be confirmed from the live site.)

## Logo

- Wordmark "Camomille" next to an abstract gold glyph (`#E3A752`).
- Glyph reads as a stylised sprout / note built from three gold strokes.

---

## Signature motifs

- **The "plus" icon cluster.** Five rounded tiles in a plus shape: a solid-gold centre holding
  the glyph, four peach arms holding line icons (lightbulb, link, puzzle, grid). This is the hero
  device and repeats across pages.
- **Soft cards.** White, rounded (~16px), gentle shadow, with a sage-green rounded "corner-tab"
  holding a white line icon. Uppercase label, gold title, ink body.
- **Folder-edge cards.** Large content cards with a thick gold border on two adjacent sides
  (a note / folder feel). Used for "Our conviction" and the pricing calculator.
- Overall: generous rounding, soft shadows, warm and minimal, with muted spot illustrations
  (teal and coral pops).

## Voice

Reassuring, plain, anti-risk. "Move forward with peace of mind." "No billing before your web app
is online." "Relax, we take the risk."

## Going AAA (Stripe-tier) — direction

Keep the warmth: gold + sage + cream is distinctive and genuinely on-brand for a "calm" identity.
Most teams would strip that out and lose the personality. We elevate instead of replace:

- A proper design-token + spacing system, with a crisp typographic scale.
- Refined type: keep rounded brand accents, tighten the body for precision.
- Gradient meshes (gold to peach to sage), soft glass cards, real depth.
- Micro-interactions, a sticky polished nav, an animated hero "plus" device.
- Real product / UI visuals in place of flat illustrations.
- Tight, confident copy with generous whitespace.
